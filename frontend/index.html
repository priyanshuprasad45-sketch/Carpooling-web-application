Our Ride Together

A Smart Ride-Sharing Solution

Introduction

Our Ride Together is a web-based platform connecting drivers and passengers for shared rides. It aims to:

  • Reduce travel costs
  • Decrease traffic congestion
  • Lower environmental impact
  • Provide secure booking and user registration

Features

User Registration

Secure login/signup system

Ride Management

Offer, search, and book rides

Booking System

Confirmation and notifications

User Profiles

Ride history and vehicle details

Project Goals

Cost Effective

Reduce travel expenses with no platform fees

Eco-Friendly

Lower carbon footprint
